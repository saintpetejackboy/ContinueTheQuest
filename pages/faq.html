Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about ContinueThe.Quest, from account setup to storytelling features.

General Questions

What is ContinueThe.Quest?

ContinueThe.Quest is a collaborative storytelling platform where fans and creators expand and continue beloved universes through community and AI-driven contributions.

Is it free to use?

Core features are free for all users. Premium options—such as advanced AI assistance and customization—can be purchased with credits earned through engagement or bought directly.

When will it launch?

We’re in the final development stages and will launch soon. Subscribe on our home page to be notified the moment we go live!

Account & Profile

How do I create an account?

Sign up with your email or use WebAuthn for passwordless login. Our system ensures secure and seamless account creation.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, from your settings. Your personal data will be removed, though contributed story content remains for narrative continuity.

What are credits?

Credits are our in-platform currency, earned by creating content and community engagement, and spent on AI features and boosts.

Content Creation

What stories can I create?

Continuations, prequels, alternate endings, parallel stories, or entirely original works across any genre.

How does collaboration work?

Branch-based contributions allow multiple narrative paths. Vote, comment, and refine story segments collaboratively.

Who owns my content?

You retain ownership of original content but grant us a display license. Collaborative works are co-owned by contributors.

Technical & Support

Supported browsers and devices?

Works best on modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge). Fully responsive on mobile; dedicated apps coming soon.

How to report bugs?

Use our feedback form or email support@continuethe.quest with details and reproduction steps.

Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information on ContinueThe.Quest.

1. Introduction

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains our practices.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: Name, email, credentials.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform.
  • Device Info: IP address, browser, OS.
  • User Content: What you submit on our platform.

3. How We Collect Information

  • Direct interactions (account creation, submissions).
  • Automated technologies (cookies, tracking).
  • Third-party sources (linked auth providers).

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • Process transactions and manage accounts.
  • Personalize your experience.
  • Communicate updates, alerts, and support.
  • Analyze usage and trends.
  • Prevent fraud and security breaches.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your data with:

  • Service providers acting on our behalf.
  • Business partners (with your consent).
  • Authorities if required by law.
  • During mergers or acquisitions.

We do not sell your personal information.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tech to enhance your experience. You can disable cookies via your browser settings.

We use both session cookies (expire on close) and persistent cookies (remain until you delete them).

7. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure.

8. Your Data Protection Rights

  • Access your personal data.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Object to processing.
  • Data portability.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.

9. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from them; if we learn we have, we will delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We'll post changes here and update the “Last updated” date—please review periodically.

11. Contact Us

Questions? Reach out at privacy@continuethe.quest.
